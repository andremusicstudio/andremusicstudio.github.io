Viola, Violin and Bass instruction

Viola, Violin instruction

The violin and viola methods are a compilation of the traditional Russian School with the flexible approach of American methodology. Ms. André has been teaching in America for the past fifteen years, and her students have won national awards and performed in state and national awards and performed in state and national honor orchestras across the country.

Bass instruction

The classical acoustical bass is taught in the traditional Italian School coupled with an American approach. Maestro André's students have attained a high level of excellence securing positions in major orchestras throughout the country.
